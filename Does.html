F.A.Q.

How does this work?

Moisturizers:
One of the fundamental pillars of skincare, moisturizers are designed to hydrate the skin and improve its texture and appearance. They contain ingredients like humectants, which attract and retain moisture, emollients that soften and smooth the skin, and occlusives that create a protective barrier to prevent water loss. By replenishing the skin's moisture levels, moisturizers help combat dryness, reduce the appearance of fine lines, and leave the skin looking supple and radiant.

How does this work?

Foundation:
Foundation is a cosmetic product applied to the face to create an even skin tone and provide coverage for imperfections. It consists of pigments, binders, and other ingredients that work together to provide the desired coverage and adherence to the skin. These ingredients help to minimize the appearance of blemishes, redness, and uneven pigmentation, giving the skin a flawless and uniform look. Foundation acts as a canvas upon which other makeup products can be applied, enhancing facial features and creating a smooth and polished appearance.

How does this work?

Mascara:
The eyes are often considered the windows to the soul, and mascara helps to emphasize their beauty. Mascara is formulated with pigments, waxes, and polymers that coat the lashes, making them appear longer, fuller, and darker. By enhancing the lashes, mascara adds definition and depth to the eyes, creating a captivating and alluring gaze. It is a simple yet powerful tool that can transform the entire face, making the eyes appear more vibrant and expressive.

How does this work?

Lipstick:
Lipstick has long been a symbol of femininity and self-expression. It adds color and enhances the appearance of the lips, making them more attractive and appealing. Lipstick formulations typically contain pigments, oils, waxes, and emollients that provide vibrant color, texture, and hydration. By applying lipstick, one can create a striking focal point on the face, exuding confidence and enhancing their overall look.
